BUILDING 21

The Vision

Building 21 is a unique new space for McGill’s diverse and talented community—students, staff, and faculty—to come together to exchange ideas, to collaborate, and to explore beyond the boundaries of disciplines, titles, or everyday work.

It is a place where creativity is encouraged, beauty and chaos are celebrated, and frontiers are meant to be ignored.

The Space

Located in the former warden’s apartment in Royal Victoria College, B21 is a place where 21st century ideas come to life in a Victorian era setting.

The space is ever-evolving, and will be continuously molded by the needs and interests of its participants.

Currently B21 houses:

  • a multi-room work-space designed to encourage creativity and collaboration (equipped with desks, lounge area, etc.)
  • new technology to explore and play with, including 2 virtual reality set-ups, arduinos, and raspberry pis
  • a kitchen for snacks and beverages to fuel the brain
Where
